# Product Brief

VachaVox is a small, paid Mac app for people who want dictation without
subscription software or technical setup.

**Promise:** Hold a key, speak, and get polished text in the app you are using.

**Price:** USD $0.99 one-time purchase.

**No:** account, subscription, backend, cloud transcription, analytics, ads,
or model browser.
